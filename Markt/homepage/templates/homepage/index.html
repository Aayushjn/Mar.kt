Done with that drafter after just one semester of use? Seeking notes for that class you "forgot" to take them for yourself? Mar.kt connects you with seniors, juniors and classmates alike and brings you a college shopping experience like no other.

Every college is different, and we at Mar.kt recognize that. Find the best second hand products listed by your peers, as well as custom items like notes, question papers etc. that you can't find anywhere else.
